Architecture Background

I am continuously seeking out creative approaches of an architectural challenge intending to improve social and economic values. At the present time I wish to extend my horizons in offering to the world visual outputs that can provide helpfull or fun enviornments. With a desire to constantly progress and learn new conceptual solutions, I oriented myself to the IT world. I have always enjoyed providing fast result and help business or fun concept to evolve.
